Our Impact

The Lesotho mission is a 10-day outreach led by Every Nation Campus (ENC) WITS, dedicated to making a lasting positive impact on young people and families. Through educational programs, mentorship, faith-based support, and practical donations, our volunteers work alongside local communities to bring hope and create meaningful change that echoes into eternity.
